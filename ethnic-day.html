Vidyuth 2025 - Ethnic Day

Event Overview

Ethnic Day at Vidyuth celebrates the rich and diverse cultural heritage of our college. Come dressed in your traditional attire, participate in cultural performances, and enjoy the vibrancy of this unique event.

Let's come together to celebrate the unity in diversity!

Event Details
